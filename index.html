Government has declared "Earth is a dead planet"






Published 22:34 MT, Thursday October 5th, 2525

Created by Onur Prasad Randrup




The Intergalactic government of Solar System One has officially declared that "Earth Prime", the original earth, is a dead and uninhabited planet. With the enormous amounts of greenhouse gases emissions that corrupt the atmosphere of "Earth Prime", it is extremely unhealthy to be living on the planet. There are still signs of living organisms on the deserted planet, but, "without proper equipment and clothing there'll be major health risks if any human decides to live on "Earth Prime" in it's current status" concluded SS1 Health and Benefits.


Expert analysises along with some of the greatest minds of the century have gathered at Mar's Environmental Gathering. They have concluded that with time and a couple small developments in technology with the understanding of biology, "Earth Prime" can and will be restored to the original thriving "blue world" that it once was.


With the the statement from the gathering, there has been a reward issued by the government towards anyone that can invent any technique/technology that can clean "Earth Prime" quickly and cost efficient. The reward number isn't stated but officals have debated it being from low numbers of 10 million upwards to possbily 500 million Solar Credits. The requirements of the invention to claim the reward are being uploaded on SS1G and SS1 Technology Inc. later during this month.


"Till further notice. 'Earth Prime' will be quarantined and any person that are below the class of Demeter, Hermes, Hephaestus, or Titan classes are forbinned to enter 'Earth Prime's' toxic atomsphere. Anyone that dares attempt to enter will be imprisoned for the act of trespassing, treason, or evasion of the law. We'll (Intergalactic Government of Solar System One) will update Solar System One of any developments."

- Representative, Helena Georgina Anupama Rye, of The Intergalactic Government of Solar System One
